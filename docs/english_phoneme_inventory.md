# English Phoneme Inventory

WaveToy now includes a compact ARPAbet-style English inventory for Articulation Lab presets.

## Vowels

Monophthongs: `IY`, `IH`, `EH`, `AE`, `AA`, `AH`, `AO`, `OW`, `UH`, `UW`, `ER`, `AX`.

Diphthongs: `AY`, `AW`, `OY`, `EY`.

Legacy compatibility aliases remain available for saved chains and examples: `EE`, `OH`, and `OO`.

## Consonants

Stops: `P`, `B`, `T`, `D`, `K`, `G`.

Affricates: `CH`, `JH`.

Fricatives: `F`, `V`, `TH`, `DH`, `S`, `Z`, `SH`, `ZH`, `H`.

Nasals: `M`, `N`, `NG`.

Liquids: `L`, `R`.

Glides: `W`, `Y`.

Each built-in preset carries an IPA label plus articulatory values such as mouth opening, tongue height/frontness, lip rounding, family, voicing, and relevant airflow/noise/closure/burst settings. These presets are intentionally lightweight synthesis controls, not a full linguistic or acoustic model.
